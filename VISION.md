# Vision

AI Evaluation Harness aims to become a vendor-neutral, open-source evaluation platform for AI systems.

The project focuses on helping engineering teams measure, validate, and monitor the quality, reliability, and performance of Large Language Model (LLM) applications.

The long-term vision is to provide a standardized approach to AI quality engineering similar to what Playwright provides for browser automation testing.

The framework should enable teams to:

* Detect regressions in AI systems.
* Measure evaluation metrics consistently.
* Compare models and prompts objectively.
* Integrate AI quality checks into CI/CD pipelines.
* Establish repeatable and explainable evaluation processes.

Success is achieved when AI evaluation becomes a first-class engineering practice rather than an ad-hoc manual activity.
